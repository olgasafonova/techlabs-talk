For Your Projects

Three moves you can make this week

01

Use AI as a thinking partner

Don't ask "write my code." Ask "here's my approach; what am I missing?" Feed it your project context. Challenge its answers. The better your input, the better the output.

Claude, ChatGPT (free tiers work)
02

Prototype in hours, not weeks

Describe what you want in plain language. Get an ugly first version. Then iterate: "add error handling," "make the API cleaner," "add tests." Ship rough, refine fast.

Cursor, Claude Code, GitHub Copilot
03

Connect AI to your stack

Pick one tool your team uses. Find or build an MCP server for it. Now AI doesn't just answer questions about your project; it can act on it.

MCP servers, n8n, APIs

The skill isn't prompting. The skill is knowing which AI output is good and which is wrong. That comes from building, not from watching tutorials.

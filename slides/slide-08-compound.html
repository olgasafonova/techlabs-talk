The Principle

It compounds

Every project teaches you something the next project needs. Every mistake sharpens your judgement. The tools change every few months. What compounds is you.

Try
Pick a real problem. Use AI to solve it. Ship something ugly.
Learn
Notice what AI got wrong. Figure out why. That's the actual skill.
Iterate
Next project goes faster because you know what to ask for and what to check.
Save
When a pattern works, save it as a skill. A reusable recipe AI follows every time.
